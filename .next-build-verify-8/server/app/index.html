History

Learn More

1991

Company Founded

Global Energy Company Limited (GEC) founded as a service contractor to major oil companies in Nigeria.

2000s

Asset-led Expansion

GEC founded GEC Petroleum Development Company Limited (GPDC), its wholly owned E&P subsidiary, and is awarded five oil and gaS blocks: OML 149 (formerly OPL 135), OML 151 (formerly OPL 2009), OPL 2010, OPL 907, and OPL 917.

2010s

Portfolio Maturation

GPDC, along with its partners, deployes over $250 million across the development of its 5 assets.

2024

Matrix Energy Partnership

Matrix Energy Exploration and Production Company Limited (Matrix) farms-in to OPL 2010 to partner GPDC to bring the block to production.

2026

35 Years Strong

GPDC celebrates 35 years of energy leadership and stewardship.
